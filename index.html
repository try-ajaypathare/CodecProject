Who We Are

Alphinix is a multi-module technology organization that works across student skill development, college collaboration, school-level robotics and IoT programs, internship and placement support, technology system development for businesses, and automation and AI-based solutions.

Our 6 Major Verticals

1. Student Technical Training

Skill development organization focused on technology fundamentals, practical implementation, and project-based learning.

2. Internship Programs

Internal & stipend-based external internship pipeline builder for real-world project exposure.

3. College Collaboration

A placement support system fostering academic-industry integration and placement assistance.

4. School Robotics & IoT

A training provider offering basic robotics systems, IoT programs, and STEM technology workshops.

5. Tech Development & Automation

A tech solutions provider for businesses, delivering websites, web apps, automation, and AI-based solutions.

6. Corporate Talent Acquisition

A hiring and talent acquisition partner supporting companies across industries in finding suitable candidates.
